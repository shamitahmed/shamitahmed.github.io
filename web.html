📊Vibe Check: AI-powered Dashboard

    What do players talk about in their reviews—and how has that changed over time? Are there patterns in the way players describe their experiences? Can we trace any shifts or trends? And more importantly, can we make sense of those changes?

    This project is a proof of concept (POC) where we explore whether psychological player experience questionnaires—like Corgis, Player Experience Inventory, Aesthemos—along with the help of LLMs, can help us better understand PX as expressed in reviews.

    Developed for the Supercell AI Hackathon 2025.
