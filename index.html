About

Hello and welcome 👋 I am Shamit, a Game Programmer from Bangladesh 🇧🇩 with 4 years of game industry experience. I am currently working as a Research Assistant and studying for my MSc degree in Game Design and Development at Aalto University, Finland 🇫🇮.

Engaging video games, immersive-interactive media and breathtaking art of various forms have always been my motivation to achieve more. I am currently focusing on making AR applications & learning Unreal Engine 5. My fields of interest:
